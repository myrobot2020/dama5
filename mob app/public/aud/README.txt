Teacher audio (offline, no dama5)

1. Copy your MP3 into this folder: public/aud/
2. The filename must match exactly the "aud_file" field in src/data/an11.16.json
   (e.g. 097_Anguttara Nikaya Book 11 116 - 1116 by Bhante Hye Dhammavuddho Mahathera.mp3)

3. Restart Vite (npm run dev). The sutta page player loads /aud/<that filename>.
